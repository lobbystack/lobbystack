It works alongside your team — handling the calls so you can focus on the work.

## What LobbyStack does

When a caller dials your LobbyStack number, the AI receptionist:

1. **Greets the caller** using your custom greeting and preferred language
2. **Answers questions** by searching your knowledge base (FAQs, documents, website content)
3. **Books appointments** by checking your calendar in real time and confirming a slot
4. **Takes messages** with caller name, phone, intent, and urgency level
5. **Transfers urgent callers** to your team via warm handoff
6. **Sends you a summary** by SMS or email after every call
