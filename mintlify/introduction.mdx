LobbyStack is an open-source AI receptionist for service businesses. It answers calls through a Twilio phone number, uses your saved business knowledge to respond to callers, can book appointments when services and Google Calendar are configured, and saves calls, messages, contacts, appointments, transcripts, and recordings in the dashboard.

<Note>
  These docs describe the features currently represented in the LobbyStack app and backend. If a feature is not documented here, treat it as not generally available yet.
</Note>

## What LobbyStack can do today

- Answer inbound calls through a configured Twilio number.
- Use a saved greeting and default customer language of English or French.
- Search knowledge from text entries, uploaded documents, and imported public website pages.
- Help callers with appointment scheduling when services, hours, staff, and Google Calendar are configured.
- Transfer a live call to a configured phone number when transfer policy allows it.
- Take callback or follow-up messages when a transfer is not possible or a caller wants staff to follow up.
- Store calls, transcripts, recordings, contacts, SMS conversations, appointments, and dashboard summaries.
- Support hosted billing and usage tracking for Free, Pro, Enterprise, and self-host plans.

## Main dashboard areas

| Area | What it is for |
| --- | --- |
| **Home** | KPIs, action-required follow-ups, upcoming appointments, and recent calls. |
| **Calls** | Inbound call history, transcripts, recordings, outcomes, and follow-up tasks. |
| **Messages** | SMS conversation history, manual replies, attachments, and AI handoff controls. |
| **Contacts** | Caller profiles, activity, appointments, blocking, and SMS consent state. |
| **Receptionist** | Greeting, language, transfer number, knowledge, services, and rules. |
| **Integrations** | Google Calendar connection and calendar selection. |
| **Settings** | Account, preferences, billing, usage, and SMS compliance setup. |
