LobbyStack supports four plan states in the app:

- **Free** for hosted workspaces with capped included usage.
- **Pro** for hosted workspaces with a monthly charge, included usage, and billable overages.
- **Enterprise** for custom hosted arrangements.
- **Self-host** for deployments that use your own providers and do not use hosted usage limits.

<Note>
  Hosted billing is powered by Polar. Use the billing page to start Pro checkout, manage your subscription, and review billing history.
</Note>

## Hosted plans

| Plan | Monthly charge | Voice minutes | Alert SMS segments | Outbound calls | Knowledge storage | Overages |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Free | $0 | 10 min | 10 | 2 | 100 MB | No |
| Pro | $15/month | 80 min | 50 | 20 | 2 GB | Yes |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom | Custom | Custom | Custom | Custom |

Pro overage rates in the current app catalog:

| Usage type | Pro overage rate |
| --- | --- |
| Voice | $0.18 per extra minute |
| Alert SMS | $0.02 per extra segment |
| Outbound calls | $0.02 per extra attempt |

## AI SMS add-on

AI SMS is represented as a Pro add-on.

| Item | Price |
| --- | --- |
| Monthly add-on | $5/month |
| One-time setup fee | $19 |
| Usage | $0.03 per SMS segment |

AI SMS also requires compliance setup before hosted business-number messaging is ready.

## Self-host

Self-hosted workspaces use your own provider accounts and are not limited by hosted plan quotas inside LobbyStack. You run the app and pay providers such as Twilio, OpenAI, Convex, hosting, email, and calendar services directly. There is no LobbyStack billing setup for a single-business self-hosted deployment.

## Manage billing

In the dashboard, open **Settings** > **Billing** to:

- See the current plan.
- Start Pro checkout.
- Open the Polar customer portal when available.
- Enable the AI SMS add-on when eligible.
- Review recent billing transactions.
- Complete SMS compliance setup for hosted AI SMS.
