---
title: "Manage contacts"
description: "Review caller profiles, interaction history, appointments, blocking status, and SMS consent."
---

The **Contacts** page lists the people connected to your calls, messages, and appointments. Contacts are created automatically from phone interactions and are updated as new activity happens.

## Contact list

Each row can show:

- Contact name or unknown contact.
- Phone number and email if available.
- Activity counts for calls and messages.
- Appointment count.
- Last interaction time.
- Blocked status.

Use search to filter by name, phone number, or email.

## Contact detail

Open a contact to view:

| Tab | What it shows |
| --- | --- |
| **Activity** | Calls, SMS messages, and appointment activity in chronological order. |
| **Appointments** | Service, staff, date and time, status, channel, and calendar sync state. |
| **Details** | Contact info, blocking state, SMS consent, and system metadata. |

## Blocking contacts

Blocking a contact stops new SMS replies and causes new inbound calls from that contact to hang up immediately. You can unblock the contact later from the same menu.

## SMS consent

The contact detail page shows whether the contact is subscribed or opted out of SMS, when the consent state changed, and the source of that change when available.

## Deleting contacts

You can delete a contact only when it does not have linked conversations or appointments. If there are linked records, LobbyStack blocks deletion to avoid orphaned history.

<Warning>
  Contact deletion is permanent when allowed. It does not bulk-delete linked conversations or appointments.
</Warning>
