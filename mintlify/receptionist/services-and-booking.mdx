---
title: "Set up services and booking"
description: "Add service entries, availability, and calendar sync so callers can book appointments through your receptionist."
---

The **Services** page contains the appointment types or offerings your receptionist can discuss with callers. Services are managed with the same entry table pattern as Knowledge and Rules: a title, content, optional tags, status, and actions.

<Note>
  For live booking, complete the full scheduling setup: services, bookable staff, business hours, service assignments, and Google Calendar. Service entries still help the receptionist answer questions even before booking is enabled.
</Note>

## Add a service

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Services">
    Go to **Receptionist** > **Services**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add service">
    Click **Add Service**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe the service">
    Enter a clear title, such as "Initial Consultation", and describe what the service includes. Add tags that match caller language if helpful.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. The service appears in the Services table.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Make services easier to book

Write service entries the way callers ask for them:

- Use the plain customer-facing service name.
- Include duration, eligibility, prep instructions, and pricing only when those details are stable.
- Add synonyms in tags when callers use different words for the same service.
- Keep unavailable or seasonal services disabled.

## Calendar booking

Google Calendar is the supported customer-facing calendar integration today. When calendar booking is configured, LobbyStack can use connected calendar data to find available times and create appointments.
