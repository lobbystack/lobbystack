---
title: "Set greeting, language, and transfers"
description: "Configure the opening line, default customer language, and default transfer number for your AI receptionist."
---

Open **Receptionist** > **AI settings** to edit the basic behavior used by the receptionist.

## Greeting

The greeting is the opening line your receptionist uses when answering a call or starting a conversation. Keep it short and specific.

Example:

> Thanks for calling Bright Smile Dental. I'm the AI receptionist. How can I help?

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open AI settings">
    In the dashboard sidebar, go to **Receptionist**. The AI settings page opens by default.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit the greeting">
    Update the **Greeting** field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** beside the greeting field.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Default customer language

The **Default customer language** setting supports:

| Option | Value |
| --- | --- |
| English | `en` |
| French | `fr` |

Choose the language your receptionist should use when the caller's preference is unknown.

<Note>
  Runtime language handling is currently built around English and French. Do not document or promise other languages unless the app adds them as supported runtime locales.
</Note>

## Default transfer number

The **Default transfer number** is the phone number used when the receptionist performs a live handoff to a human.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter a number">
    Add a complete phone number in the transfer number field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** beside the transfer number field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add rules if needed">
    Use **Receptionist** > **Rules** to describe when the receptionist should transfer, take a message, or ask for callback details.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  If no transfer number is saved, live transfers cannot complete. The receptionist can still take a callback or follow-up message.
</Warning>
