The **Integrations** page is where LobbyStack connects with the tools your team already uses, from calendars to CRMs and communication apps.

Each integration guide explains what the connection does, what access it needs, and how to confirm it is working.

## Google Calendar

Connect Google Calendar when you want LobbyStack to help callers book appointments. After you authorize Google, choose the calendar that should receive bookings.

## Where to find it

Open **Manage** > **Integrations** from the dashboard sidebar.
