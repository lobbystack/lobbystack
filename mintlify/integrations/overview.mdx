The **Integrations** page is where you connect tools outside LobbyStack. Use it to connect Google Calendar for appointment availability and booking sync, and to review SMS-related setup.

## Google Calendar

Connect Google Calendar when you want LobbyStack to help callers book appointments. After you authorize Google, choose the calendar that should receive bookings.

## SMS

SMS behavior is split between system alerts, manual operator replies, and AI SMS. Hosted AI SMS is available on Pro with the AI SMS add-on and approved compliance setup.

## Where to find it

Open **Manage** > **Integrations** from the dashboard sidebar.
