LobbyStack includes two distinct SMS features. Every plan includes alert SMS — automated text summaries sent to you after each call so you never miss what happened while you were busy. The two-way AI SMS add-on goes further: it lets the AI have full text conversations with your callers, follow up after missed calls, and send appointment confirmations — all without you doing anything.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Alert SMS">

## Alert SMS

Alert SMS is included on all plans — Free, Pro, and Enterprise — at no extra charge. After every call, LobbyStack sends a text message to your verified phone number with a structured summary of what happened: caller name, phone number, their reason for calling, and any key details the AI captured.

### What you receive

Each alert SMS includes:

- Caller name and phone number
- The purpose of the call (booking request, question, urgent issue, etc.)
- A brief summary of the conversation
- Any follow-up action flagged by the AI

### How to enable alert SMS

Alert SMS is on by default. To receive messages, your phone number must be verified in your account settings.

1. Go to **Settings** in your dashboard.
2. Confirm your phone number is listed and verified.
3. If your number is not yet verified, follow the on-screen prompts to add and verify it.

Once your number is verified, you will receive a summary text after every call automatically.

<Note>
Alert SMS segments are included in your monthly plan allowance. Free plan includes 10 segments per month; Pro includes 50 segments per month, then $0.02 per additional segment. A long summary may use more than one segment.
</Note>

## Two-way AI SMS

Two-way AI SMS is an optional add-on available exclusively on the **Pro plan**. It enables the AI to send and receive text messages on your behalf — not just notify you, but actively converse with your customers.

<Note>
Two-way AI SMS requires a **Pro plan** subscription. It is not available on the Free plan. You can upgrade from **Settings > Billing**.
</Note>

### What two-way AI SMS does

With this add-on enabled, your AI receptionist can:

- **Follow up after missed calls** — automatically send a text to callers you couldn't answer in time
- **Handle inbound SMS** — respond to customers who text your LobbyStack number directly
- **Send appointment confirmations** — text a confirmation to the caller after a booking is made on a call
- **Continue conversations** — answer follow-up questions from customers over text, using your knowledge base

### Pricing

| Item | Cost |
|---|---|
| Monthly add-on fee | $5/month |
| One-time setup fee | $19 |
| Per segment | $0.03/segment |

A segment is a unit of SMS billing (roughly 160 characters for standard messages). Most short conversational messages are one segment.

### How to add two-way AI SMS

1. Make sure you are on the **Pro plan**. If you are on Free, go to **Settings > Billing** to upgrade.
2. From **Settings > Billing**, find the **Two-way AI SMS** add-on and click **Add**.
3. Review the pricing and confirm the $19 setup fee and $5/month recurring charge.
4. Once activated, the AI will begin handling inbound SMS and sending follow-ups automatically.

### SMS compliance and opt-out

Opt-out is handled automatically. When a customer replies STOP to any text from your LobbyStack number, they are immediately removed from further AI-initiated messages. You do not need to manage this manually.
