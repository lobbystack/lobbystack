title: "Connect Google Calendar"
description: "Connect a Google account, choose a writable calendar, and let LobbyStack use it for appointment availability and booking sync."
---

Google Calendar lets LobbyStack read calendar availability, create booking events, and track calendar sync state for appointments.

<Note>
  Connect Google Calendar as part of your booking setup, along with services, staff, and business hours.
</Note>

## How the integration works

LobbyStack uses Google OAuth. You connect a Google account, LobbyStack stores the connection securely, and you select the calendar that should receive bookings. The integration can also sync busy time so LobbyStack avoids offering unavailable slots.

## Connect Google Calendar

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Integrations">
    In the dashboard, open **Integrations**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Google connection">
    Find **Google Calendar** and click **Connect**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize Google">
    Sign in to Google and approve the requested calendar permissions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a calendar">
    After returning to LobbyStack, click the connected Google card, choose a calendar from the dropdown, and click **Save calendar**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Change the selected calendar

1. Open **Integrations**.
2. Click the Google Calendar card.
3. Choose another calendar.
4. Click **Save calendar**.

LobbyStack uses the newly selected calendar for future sync and booking work.

## Reconnect or disconnect

If Google access expires or is revoked, the card shows that reconnect is required. Click **Reconnect Google** and complete OAuth again.

To remove the integration, open the Google Calendar card and click **Disconnect**. LobbyStack removes the current connection for bookings.
