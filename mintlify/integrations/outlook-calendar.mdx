LobbyStack plans to support Microsoft 365 and Outlook Calendar with the same real-time booking flow available for Google Calendar today. When a caller asks for an appointment, the AI will check your Outlook calendar for open slots and book directly — no manual entry required.

<Info>
**Outlook Calendar integration is not yet available.** The option appears in **Settings > Integrations**, but the **Connect** button is currently disabled. This feature is on the roadmap and will be enabled in a future release.
</Info>

## What Outlook Calendar will do

When the integration launches, it will work the same way as the Google Calendar integration:

- The AI checks your Outlook or Microsoft 365 calendar in real time during every call
- Open slots are offered to the caller based on your availability
- Confirmed appointments are written directly to your calendar
- You select which calendar to use after connecting your Microsoft account
- Connection status is shown in **Settings > Integrations**, with options to reconnect or disconnect

The setup will use Microsoft's OAuth flow, so you authorize access once from your LobbyStack dashboard with no manual configuration.

## How to be notified when it's available

There are two ways to stay up to date:

- **Dashboard**: Check **Settings > Integrations** — the Outlook Calendar card will become active when the integration is released
- **GitHub**: Watch the [LobbyStack repository](https://github.com/morencyr/LobbyStack) for release notes and updates

## Use Google Calendar in the meantime

If you need calendar-based booking today, Google Calendar is fully supported. The setup takes a few minutes and provides the same real-time availability and booking experience that Outlook Calendar will offer.
