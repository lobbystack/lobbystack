This guide follows the onboarding flow in the LobbyStack web app.

<Note>
  No credit card is required to create an account. Hosted usage limits are shown in **Settings** after you sign in.
</Note>

## Before you begin

You need:

- An email address and password.
- A mobile phone number that can receive an SMS verification code.
- A public website URL if you want LobbyStack to import website knowledge during onboarding.

## Onboarding steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to the LobbyStack sign-up page, enter your email address and password, and submit the form.

    LobbyStack uses email and password authentication. After signup, you are taken into onboarding.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify your mobile number">
    Enter a mobile number in international format and request a verification code. Enter the code you receive by SMS.

    The verified number helps LobbyStack suggest a local business number later in onboarding.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Import your website, or skip for now">
    Enter a public website URL to start a background website import, or choose **Skip for now**.

    Website import crawls public pages such as services, about, FAQ, hours, and contact pages. Private pages, local network URLs, and login-only pages are not imported.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Claim a phone number">
    Review the suggested Twilio number or click **Pick a different number** to search by city, area code, or toll-free number.

    Click **Continue** to claim the selected number for your business. LobbyStack configures the number's voice webhook for your receptionist.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Finish receptionist setup">
    Open **Receptionist** and review the greeting, default customer language, transfer number, knowledge, services, and rules before sending production traffic to the number.
  </Step>
</Steps>
