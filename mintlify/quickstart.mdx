LobbyStack is designed so that any service business owner can go from signup to a live AI receptionist without any technical help. This guide walks you through every step of the onboarding flow so you know exactly what to expect.

<Note>
  No credit card is required to start. Your free account includes 10 voice minutes, 2 outbound call attempts, and 10 SMS alert segments.
</Note>

## Before you begin

You need:

- An email address and a password you'd like to use
- A mobile phone number you can receive a text on (used to suggest a local number for your business)

## Onboarding steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to the LobbyStack sign-up page and enter your email address and a password. LobbyStack uses email and password authentication — there is no third-party login required.

    Once you submit the form, you are signed in and taken directly into onboarding.

    See [Create and configure your LobbyStack account](/getting-started/account-setup) for details on managing your account settings after signup.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify your mobile number">
    LobbyStack asks for your mobile phone number and sends a one-time verification code by SMS. Enter the code to confirm your number.

    Your verified number is used in the next step to suggest a local business number with a matching area code. It is not shared with callers.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Claim your dedicated phone number">
    LobbyStack suggests a local number based on your verified phone's area code. You can accept the suggestion or search by city, area code, or choose a toll-free number instead.

    Once you confirm, that number is reserved exclusively for your business. Callers will dial this number to reach your AI receptionist. If you already have a business number, you can forward calls from it to your LobbyStack number.

    See [Claim a dedicated phone number](/getting-started/claim-your-number) for full details on number options and call forwarding.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Import your website (optional)">
    LobbyStack can scan your business website and use the content to answer caller questions — services, hours, pricing, location, and more. Enter your website URL and LobbyStack will import it automatically.

    You can skip this step and add website content later from your dashboard, or add information manually through the knowledge base.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Your receptionist goes live">
    After you claim your number, your AI receptionist is active immediately. Any call to your LobbyStack number is answered by the AI. You can customize your greeting, call-handling rules, and knowledge base at any time from the dashboard without taking the receptionist offline.
  </Step>
</Steps>
