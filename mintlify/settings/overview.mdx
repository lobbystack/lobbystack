The **Settings** page contains workspace and account preferences. It is organized into Usage, Billing, Team, and Preferences.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Usage" icon="chart-line" href="/billing/usage">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Billing" icon="credit-card" href="/billing/plans">
    Review plan options, add-ons, and subscription actions.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Settings pages

| Page | What it is for |
| --- | --- |
| **Usage** | Current billing-period usage, remaining included usage, reset timing, and metered usage. |
| **Billing** | Current plan, upgrades, subscription management, AI SMS add-on, and SMS compliance setup. |
| **Team** | Business name, account email, and password changes. |
| **Preferences** | Dashboard language, time format, and light or dark theme. |

## Update account details

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    In the dashboard sidebar, go to **Manage** > **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a settings page">
    Use the tabs at the top of Settings to open **Usage**, **Billing**, **Team**, or **Preferences**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make your change">
    Update the field or action you need. Some changes, such as email updates, require confirmation.
  </Step>
</Steps>
