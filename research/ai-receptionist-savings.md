# Research: AI Receptionist Savings

Topic: How much can a small business save using an AI receptionist?

Audience: Small businesses that depend on phone calls for bookings, quotes, appointments, orders, or support.

## Useful sources

- BLS Occupational Outlook Handbook, Receptionists: https://www.bls.gov/ooh/Office-and-Administrative-Support/Receptionists.htm
  - Median hourly wage for receptionists was $17.90 in May 2024.
  - This is wage only. It does not include payroll taxes, benefits, hiring, training, sick coverage, or management time.
- Ruby plans and pricing: https://www.ruby.com/plans-and-pricing/
  - Public virtual receptionist plans shown during research: 50 minutes for $250/month, 100 minutes for $395/month, 200 minutes for $720/month, 500 minutes for $1,725/month.
  - Useful as a live receptionist comparison point, not a direct AI receptionist comparison.
- Smith.ai AI receptionist pricing page: https://smith.ai/pricing/ai-receptionist
  - Useful for market framing around AI receptionist cost comparisons and included features.
- LobbyStack missed call revenue calculator:
  - Route: /missed-call-revenue-calculator/
  - Formula: missed calls per week x 4.3 x opportunity rate x booking rate x average job/order value.

## Article angle

The post should avoid claiming that AI "saves" every dollar shown by a missed-call calculation. Better framing:

1. Cost savings: what the business may spend less on human coverage, after-hours answering, or manual admin interruption.
2. Revenue protection: the revenue currently at risk because calls go unanswered or follow-up happens too late.
3. Net savings: recovered revenue plus avoided cost, minus software cost and overages.

## Example math

Conservative example for a small business:

- 8 missed calls per week
- 40% are real opportunities
- 30% would have booked
- $300 average booking/order value

Monthly revenue at risk:

8 x 4.3 x 0.40 x 0.30 x 300 = $1,238.40

If better answering recovers 25% to 50% of that leak, the business could protect about $310 to $619/month before subtracting the AI receptionist plan cost.
