You can now keep a clearer set of instructions for how LobbyStack answers, qualifies, and routes calls. The updated rule editor focuses on practical call-handling behavior, so business owners can review the receptionist's priorities without digging through setup details.

The goal is simple: make the assistant easier to change when your business changes. Update the way it greets callers, handles urgent requests, explains services, or decides when a person should step in.

We also tightened the surrounding setup flow so the receptionist can use those rules consistently across live calls, summaries, and follow-up work.
