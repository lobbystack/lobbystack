LobbyStack now writes call summaries with a stronger focus on what happened, what the caller wanted, and what should happen next. That makes it easier to review missed opportunities, urgent requests, new leads, and appointments without replaying every recording.

Follow-up notes are also more consistent. When a call needs human attention, the dashboard highlights the useful context first: caller details, intent, urgency, and the action your team should consider.

This update is part of a broader push to make the operator dashboard feel calmer during high-call-volume days.
