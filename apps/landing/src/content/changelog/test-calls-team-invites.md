We added two features for teams getting LobbyStack ready for real callers: test calls for your AI receptionist and member invites for your business workspace.

You can now test call your AI receptionist directly in LobbyStack. Start a live call from the dashboard, hear the greeting, check the business details it uses, and tune the instructions before customers reach the number.

You can also invite team members to your business workspace. Bring in the people who help with calls, booking, setup, or billing, then keep the work in one LobbyStack business.
